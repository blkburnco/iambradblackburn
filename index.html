Animation & Illustration

Animation is the art of making things move, visually and emotionally.

Animation and Illustration are effective tools to tell stories. Much of this percolates through in 'explainer videos', but that is just the tip of the ice berg. I specialize in creating 2D/3D animation that include unique and custom illustration fit for the specific project. During my career, I have developed animated packages for companies like GE, Delta, and many other lesser known brands.

Branding &
Print Design

Branding is an identity and a presentation expressed through a person, product, principle, pledge, pursuit, partnership or program.

You have the opportunity to act first and give your brand the meaning that you want it to have; guiding the communication and experience from interaction before allowing others to draw conclusions outside of your influence.

My maxim in branding is simple...”Think”. While everyone else is out there drawing up designs for logos, websites and other advertisements, I am focused on thinking. Why? Because the better you understand how you think, and how the people you are trying to reach think, the better and more effectively you can communicate with them. That’s my goal. Let’s create something meaningful that makes a difference.

“Quality means doing it right when no one is looking” – Henry Ford

UI / UX Design

Good design is the result of great listening. The designer’s notebook holds clues to a passion and vision — secrets that are translated as the craftsman carefully selects and stitches together the raw materials—fonts, colors, and images.

Often, the process of 'translating' information into the appropriate format gets short-changed and the outcome becomes lackluster. As an artist and designer, my approach towards creating interfaces and user experiences begins in understanding how people interact, comprehend and absorb information in the world around them. This process involves research, testing and iteration to get right, but when done appropriately, the results can be astounding.

“It takes a great man to be a good listener.” – Calvin Coolidge
