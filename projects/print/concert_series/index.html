Concert Series Brochure

Each year, Shell Point Retirement Community presents a series of concerts in the fall and winter. During my tenure there, I came up with a themed campaign for 3 series. This particular one is taken from the 2015-2016 season that I dubbed "Music in Motion". My goal here was to create a style that was very rich in color and directed by the passion of the music. The collateral that was created included the following brochure, program guide, artist inserts, tickets and ads for numerous news and lifestyle publications.
